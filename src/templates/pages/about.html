About page


Group works and team projects are important and necessary in our academic area, also in the future work. However, finding ideal people to work with is difficult, especially when people come to a new place where they do not know each other. It would be helpful if, when people need to find partners for the group projects, we had a place where people can submit the recruition information, so that people could efficiently find the group members they want to work with.

Our goal is to build a web-app that conveniently shares group events and allows people to find desirable projects to join in. We will develop a full-featured web application for posting and sharing group events of Oberlin students.
